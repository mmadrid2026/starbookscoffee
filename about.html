ABOUT US

Established in 2022, Starbooks Coffee aims to provide a sense of comfort and solace to bibliophiles and coffee connoisseurs. We provide a modest variety of new books together with a carefully chosen collection of high-quality secondhand books, with an emphasis on academic publishers, literature, the arts, humanities, sciences, and other fields. Whether it's bread, cakes, and pastries, or just a bottomless cup of coffee, Starbooks Coffee surely is the place to be.
